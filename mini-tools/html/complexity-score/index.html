HCCP scoring

As Community Paediatricians we often discuss the increasing medical complexity of the children we see, but have found it difficult to demonstrate this in a simple way.

The Health Complexity in Community Paediatrics (HCCP) Score offers a rapid, reliable, easy to use measurement, that assesses acrice use.mber of domains, much like the APGAR score used for neonates at delivery. It has been shown to predict acute health service use.

This is useful for scoring health complexity in children known to community paediatric teams, supporting individual care plans and health systems governance and structuring.
